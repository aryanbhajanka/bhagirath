Welcome to Bhagirath Water Atomization Project

Smart Automation for Water Conservation

About the Project

The Bhagirath project focuses on developing high-efficiency water atomizers using Autodesk Fusion 360. Our goal is to reduce water inlet consumption by up to 90%, making the atomizer an eco-friendly solution for industries and households. This project was part of the Smart India Hackathon 2024 under the Smart Automation theme.

Feasibility and Viability

Risks and Challenges

  • Material Durability: Fusion 360's material library is used to select long-lasting, cost-effective materials.
  • Production Delays: Toolpath optimization and CAM tools in Fusion 360 streamline manufacturing to minimize delays.
  • Market Adoption: The project highlights water-saving benefits to attract eco-conscious consumers.
  • Compatibility Issues: Parametric design ensures compatibility with various tap systems.

The design is optimized for mass production, with cost-effective and durable materials selected using Fusion 360's tools. CAM and simulation capabilities ensure efficient manufacturing, reducing production time, while quick prototyping is supported by Fusion 360's 3D printing capabilities.

Impact and Benefits

Our water atomization technology offers several key benefits:

  • Significant water conservation through efficient atomization, reducing water consumption by up to 90%.
  • Cost-effective and scalable for mass production using CNC or injection molding.
  • Eco-friendly solution appealing to industries and households looking to save water and reduce costs.
